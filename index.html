About the Teacher

I'm Afsar Khan — Scholar, Teacher, and your Guide through this Website

I'm a Ph.D. scholar in English Literature and a passionate educator. My background in journalism and mass communication taught me how to write with clarity, while my love for literature showed me the path to teaching.

For the past decade, I've been guiding students through English literature — in offline classrooms, online lessons, and on my YouTube channel, AK Explained. On this website, I write notes in both English and Hinglish for students preparing for their exams — from BA and MA to NET/JRF, PGT/TGT and UPSC.

My goal is simple: to break down complex concepts — critical theory, poetry, drama, and prose — into explanations any student can understand and walk into an exam with confidence. What you'll find here is clarity, depth, and language that feels like your own — free, forever.

Let's make literature a little more enjoyable, together.
