What I can and cannot do

Yes, gladly

  • Subject doubts about a text, a term or a theory
  • Corrections to anything on this site

  • Guidance on preparation, sequence and books

No, honestly

  • Writing your assignment or dissertation for you
  • Paid one-to-one tuition — there is no time for it
  • Predicting the paper or sharing "important questions" leaks
  • Promotional and collaboration offers from unrelated businesses

I never ask anyone for money and I do not run any paid group. If an account claiming to be AK Explained asks you for a payment, it is not me — please tell me about it.
